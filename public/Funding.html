Funding Support

You could be missing out on money which you never have to pay back.
Use our Grants Search tool and we'll match you to grants for which you may be eligible.

We offer various funding options for home students to support their educational journey. Please use the eligibility check form below to see what funding options may be available to you.

Eligibility Check

Where Do These Grants Come From?

Registered charitable organisations across the UK give grants to assist people in need.
Grants are given to people based on their personal circumstances, for example, a job you've worked at or a health condition you may have.

Who Can Apply?

Everyone can apply for a grant and many charities will help people who cannot claim welfare benefits because of their status in the UK. We can’t guarantee you’ll get a grant but we can show you what you could apply for.

How Will I Be Paid?

Each fund’s payment methods will be specific to them. Grants can be given as money, products,
or services that don't have to be paid back.

When Will I Receive an Answer?

Each fund has a different timeframe. This can range anywhere from 5 days to 3 months. Where we have this information, we show it in the grant fund details.

Before You Start

Remember, all your answers are private and never shared with anyone. The more information you put into the tool, the better the chance of finding grants you could be eligible for.

When answering the Grants Search questions, it is important to remember that you can consider the situations of either yourself, your partner, or your immediate family members.

For example, if an employment or health question does not apply to you personally, you might qualify for a certain grant because of your immediate family member’s work or health situation – even if they are no longer alive.

  • Partner refers to someone you are married to, have a civil partnership with or live with as a couple.
  • Immediate family member means your partner, parents or dependent children.

You can skip any of the questions while filling out the Grants Search, however the more information you include will increase the number of grants we can match you with.
